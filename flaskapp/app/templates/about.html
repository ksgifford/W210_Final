Privacy Policy

Photos uploaded to CritterCounter are held on a private S3 bucket on Amazon Web Services. Users have the option to purge their data after classification, entirely removing it from CritterCounter databases and file storage. If desired, photos can be shared as open source and to help build out species range maps and add to our classification training data set.
